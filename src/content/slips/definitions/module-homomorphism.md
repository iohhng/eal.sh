---
title: Module Homomorphism
---

Let $R$ be a ring and $M, N$ be $R$-[modules](def:module). An $R$-module *homomorphism* is a function $f \colon M \to N$ such that 
$$
f(r \cdot m_{1} + s \cdot m_{2}) = r \cdot f(m_{1}) + s \cdot f(m_{2})
$$
for all $r, s \in R$ and $m_{1}, m_{2} \in M$.
