---
title: Module
aliases: ['left-module']
---

Let $R$ be a ring. A (*left*) *$R$-module* is an abelian group $M$ equipped with a map $R \times M \to M$, denoted $(r, m) \mapsto r \cdot m$, which satisfies:

:::enumerate{style="(i)"}
1. $r \cdot (m + n) = r \cdot m + r \cdot n$;
2. $r \cdot (s \cdot m) = (rs) \cdot m$;
3. $(r + s) \cdot m = r \cdot m + s \cdot m$; and
4. $1 \cdot m = m$
:::

for all $r, s \in R$ and $m, n \in M$.
